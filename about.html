About us

Manchester Boxing Club is based in Manchester city centre, UK. We aim to deliver maximum benefits you'd get from a community gym but with a friendly, competitive dimension of having someone to train alongside. We open throughout the day which is perfect for those with flexible or difficult working hours. All our classes are small, fit for ages from 10 to 60. Our friendly boxing coaches will help you gain the most from your sessions, eliminating bad habits and ensuring you optimise your time in the gym. We pride ourselves on our results and always make sure that you are training hard and achieving your individual goals.
